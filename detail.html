Synopsis

In a future where Earth is becoming uninhabitable, a team of astronauts embarks on a perilous journey through a newly discovered wormhole near Saturn. Their mission: to find a new habitable planet for humanity. As they venture into the unknown depths of space, they face unimaginable challenges that test the limits of human endurance and scientific understanding.

Led by former NASA pilot Cooper (David Mitchell) and brilliant physicist Dr. Brand (Emma Richardson), the team must navigate not only the physical dangers of space travel but also the mind-bending implications of relativity, where time passes differently across different worlds. What begins as a mission to save humanity becomes a profound exploration of love, sacrifice, and the bonds that transcend space and time.
